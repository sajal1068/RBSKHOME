About The Program

Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram

RBSK is an innovative initiative for early identification of selected health conditions and linking them for medical and surgical management, providing comprehensive care for children in the community.

Screening Process

  • i. Comprehensive Newborn Screening (CNS): Performed at delivery points to screen for birth defects in newborns.
  • ii. Community Screening: Conducted by Mobile Health Teams (MHT) in Anganwadis and Government schools.

Management & Referral

  • District Early Intervention Centers (DEIC): Comprehensive management and care under one roof.
  • Tertiary Care: Specialized surgical management and advanced interventions like Cochlear implants.
