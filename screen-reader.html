Screen Reader Access

The Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram (RBSK) portal complies with World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 level AA. This will enable people with visual impairments to access the portal using assistive technologies, such as screen readers.
