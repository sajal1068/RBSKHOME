Welcome to RBSK Portal

Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram (RBSK) is an innovative initiative aiming at early identification and early intervention for children from birth to 18 years to cover 4 ‘D’s viz. Defects at birth, Deficiencies, Diseases, Development delays including disability.
