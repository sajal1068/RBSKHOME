Detailed Framework

Program Overview & Structure

01

Universal Screening

Bharat ke har bacche (0-18 years) ki screening ki jati hai. Ismein ghar par (ASHA), Anganwadi aur sarkari schoolon mein Mobile Health Teams (MHT) dwara jaanch shamil hai.

02

Early Intervention

Jaise hi kisi vikaar (4Ds) ka pata chalta hai, bacche ko turant **DEIC (District Early Intervention Centre)** refer kiya jata hai jahan experts ki team unka management karti hai.

03

Tertiary Care

Major surgeries jaise Cardiac ya Cochlear Implants ke liye bacchon ko bade medical institutes mein refer kiya jata hai, jiska poora kharch sarkaar uthati hai.
