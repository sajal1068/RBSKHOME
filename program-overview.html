Program Overview & Structure

A comprehensive collection of the latest official guidelines, circulars, and reports issued by the Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram (RBSK).

Operational Workflow

STEP 01

Screening

Mobile Health Teams visit schools and Anganwadis for check-ups.

STEP 02

Referral

Identified cases are referred to District Early Intervention Centres.

STEP 03

Management

Therapeutic care and specialist consultation at DEIC facilities.

STEP 04

Treatment

Free surgeries and medical interventions at tertiary hospitals.
