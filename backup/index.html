About the Program

Comprehensive Care from
Birth to 18 Years.

Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram (RBSK) is a pioneering initiative for early identification of health conditions and linking them for medical and surgical management, providing holistic care for every child in the community.

4 D's Covered

Early Detection
for Better Health

Screening Process

  • CNS Newborn screening at delivery points for birth defects.
  • MHT Community screening at Anganwadis & Schools.

Management

  • ✔ DEIC: Care under one roof at Districts.
  • ✔ Tertiary: Specialized surgical management.
